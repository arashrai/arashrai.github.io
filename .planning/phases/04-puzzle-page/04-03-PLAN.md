    Complete puzzle page at narsh2026/puzzles/ with:
    1. 4x5 card grid puzzle game (Clues by Sam format) -- 20 characters, criminal/innocent guessing, card flip with clue text
    2. Color tagging system (right-click desktop, long-press mobile) for player note-taking
    3. Inspect/dim mode to gray out already-processed cards
    4. Progressive hint system ("Show Hint" / "Show More" / "Hide Hint")
    5. Timer tracking solve time, pauses when browser tab is hidden
    6. Share results (emoji grid with stats copied to clipboard)
    7. Wrong guess modal ("Not so fast!") for non-deducible guesses
    8. Game complete modal ("Case Closed!") with stats and share/restart
    9. Reset confirmation modal ("Start Over?") with destructive action warning
    10. localStorage persistence -- game progress survives page refresh
    11. Daily Puzzles We Love section with 5 curated link cards (Clues by Sam, Minute Cryptic, Globle, Wordle, FoodGuessr)
    12. Warm wedding aesthetic matching site design (cream, terracotta, Playfair Display + Source Sans 3)
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Open http://localhost:8000/narsh2026/puzzles/ (or your local dev server) after logging in with a valid password.

    **Core Gameplay:**
    1. See a 4x5 grid of 20 character cards (emoji face, name, profession)
    2. Tap a card -- a Criminal/Innocent guess dialog appears
    3. Guess correctly -- card flips with 3D animation, reveals role + clue text, speech bubble appears briefly
    4. Guess incorrectly on a deducible card -- card shakes, Mistakes counter increments
    5. Guess on a non-deducible card -- "Not so fast!" modal explains to flip more cards first

    **Features:**
    6. Right-click a card (desktop) or long-press (mobile) -- color tag dot appears at top-right, cycles through colors on repeat
    7. Click "Show Hint" -- suggested cards pulse with a shadow glow
    8. Click "Inspect" -- button says "Inspecting"; click any flipped card to dim it (opacity drops)
    9. Timer in stats bar counts up; switch to another tab and back -- timer should pause and resume
    10. Complete the puzzle or click "Share Results" -- "Copied!" feedback, paste in a text editor to see emoji grid

    **Persistence & Reset:**
    11. Refresh the page mid-game -- all progress should be restored (flipped cards, color tags, stats)
    12. Click "Play Again" -- confirmation asks "Start Over?"; confirm resets everything

    **Daily Links:**
    13. Scroll below the puzzle -- "Daily Puzzles We Love" section with 5 link cards
    14. Click any link -- opens external puzzle site in a new tab

    **Visual & Responsive:**
    15. Check that the page matches the warm wedding aesthetic (cream background, terracotta accents, correct fonts)
    16. Resize to mobile width (or check on phone) -- grid stays 4 columns, cards resize, controls remain usable

    **Keyboard:**
    17. Tab through cards and controls -- all navigable with keyboard
    18. Press Escape on any modal -- it closes
  </how-to-verify>
